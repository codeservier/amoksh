Career

Volunteers Opportunities

Volunteering with DCT allows individuals to actively contribute to the mission of improving access to health services and quality education. Volunteers are the backbone of our initiatives, and we offer various opportunities to engage: Healthcare Outreach: Volunteers can participate in community health drives, assist medical professionals, and help with health education programs in underprivileged areas. Educational Support: Volunteers can help with tutoring students, assisting teachers in classrooms, organizing educational workshops, and distributing learning materials. Event Coordination: Help us organize and manage events, fundraising campaigns, or awareness programs. Volunteers can assist with planning, logistics, and outreach. Community Engagement: Work directly with communities to raise awareness about health and education, gather feedback, and support local initiatives aimed at improving living standards.

Associates

For those seeking a deeper, long-term involvement, joining DCT as an associate provides the opportunity to work more closely on strategic projects and organizational growth: Project Managers: Lead projects focused on health or education, ensuring successful implementation, overseeing budgets, and coordinating with local partners and stakeholders. Fundraising Associates: Develop fundraising strategies, create grant proposals, and work with donors to secure funding for DCT’s programs. Health and Education Specialists: Professionals in the fields of healthcare or education can contribute their expertise to guide program development, create training modules, and improve service delivery in communities. Administrative and Technical Roles: Join the team to provide essential administrative support or technical skills in areas like IT, finance, and HR, ensuring the smooth functioning of the organization.

Why Get Involved?

Make a Real Impact: Every role, whether volunteer or associate, contributes directly to improving lives through health and education initiatives. Develop New Skills: Volunteering and working with DCT provide opportunities to gain valuable experience, learn new skills, and grow both personally and professionally. Be Part of a Passionate Community: Join like-minded individuals who are committed to the cause and build lifelong connections while giving back.

Program Associates

DCT offers various professional and long-term opportunities for individuals who want to make a lasting impact in the fields of health and education. As a Program Associate, you will play a key role in driving our initiatives forward, contributing your skills, expertise, and passion to create meaningful change

Why Join DCT as a Program Associate

Professional Growth: DCT provides a supportive environment for personal and professional development, offering mentorship and learning opportunities. Meaningful Work: You will contribute to life-changing programs that improve health and education for underserved communities, making a direct, positive impact on people’s lives. Collaborative Environment: Work with a passionate, dedicated team of professionals and volunteers who are committed to our cause. Long-Term Opportunities: Many Program Associates start in one role and grow into leadership positions, expanding their responsibilities and contributions to the organization.

Who We Are Looking For

Passionate about making a difference in health and education. Skilled in their respective fields with relevant qualifications and experience. Dedicated to long-term involvement with a willingness to work in diverse and sometimes challenging environments. Collaborative and capable of working in a team, but also proactive and independent when needed.

How to Apply

If you are interested in joining DCT as a Program Associate, please submit your resume and a cover letter outlining your experience and why you are passionate about working with DCT. Send your application to careers@DCT.org. Make sure to specify the role you are applying for in the subject line of your email. Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis, and only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about the available roles or the application process, feel free to reach out to our HR Team at careers@DCT.org or call us at [Phone Number]. By joining DCT as a Program Associate, you will be contributing your expertise to an organization dedicated to transforming lives through health and education initiatives. We look forward to welcoming passionate individuals who are ready to make a lasting impact.
